HibouNT Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 19, 2025

HibouNT is a minimalist new tab extension focused on user privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All settings and customizations remain on your device.

Data we do not collect

  • Personal information (name, email, identifiers)
  • Browsing history or page contents
  • Cookies, analytics, or tracking data

Data storage

Any preferences you set within HibouNT are stored locally in your browser (e.g., local storage or extension storage). This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

HibouNT requests only the minimum permissions required for its features. For example, the tabs permission is used to enhance your new tab experience and is not used to collect or transmit your browsing data.

Third‑party services

HibouNT does not use third‑party analytics, advertising networks, trackers, or external telemetry.

Children’s privacy

HibouNT does not target or knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continuing to use the extension after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about privacy, contact us at hiboudevinfo@gmail.com, or contact me on Discord: .hibou
